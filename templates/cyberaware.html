About Cyber Awareness

Why Cyber Awareness Matters

In today's digital world, cyber attacks are increasing rapidly. Hackers use phishing emails, fake websites, malware, scams, and social engineering techniques to steal personal information, passwords, and financial data.

Cyber awareness helps students, businesses, employees, and internet users understand modern cyber threats and learn how to stay safe online through proper security practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread cybersecurity awareness and teach practical online safety techniques that help people secure their devices, accounts, social media, and digital identity from cybercriminals.
